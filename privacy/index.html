Sub Sook Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 May 2026

Summary

Sub Sook syncs your data to the cloud (Supabase) under your account for both Free and Sub Sook Premium. A local SQLite copy on your device supports offline use. Sign-in with Apple or Google is required.

Account & sign-in

Authentication uses Supabase. When you sign in with Apple or Google we receive your provider user ID and email (if you share it through the provider's consent screen). We do not store your password and we do not receive your social-graph data (friends list, posts, photos). When signing in with Apple we additionally generate a random nonce; the nonce is sent (hashed) to Apple and verified by Supabase to prevent token replay.

Cloud Sync (all plans)

After sign-in, all subscription and expense data syncs to Supabase automatically — Free and Premium alike. The app keeps a local copy and syncs when you are back online.

Data fields

  • Item names, amounts, billing dates, categories, tags, shared members, payment history, budget settings, display name
  • Stored on cloud sync and cached locally on your device

Exchange rates, notifications, biometric lock, widget

FX from open.er-api.com; local notifications; biometric on device only; iOS Premium widget uses App Group on device.

Sub Sook Premium

RevenueCat / Apple / Google for billing. Premium adds exports and higher limits — not exclusive cloud sync.

Deleting data

Item deletes sync to cloud. Uninstall removes local copy. Sign out on Profile. Delete account and all data (cloud + device): Privacy Policy → “Delete all my data” at the bottom of Deleting data. Support: subsook@proton.me
